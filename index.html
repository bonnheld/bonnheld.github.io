About Our Team

This is a branch of Zhengzhou Institute of Surveying and Mapping, which is a leading research team dedicated to advancing the frontiers of geospatial science, positioning technology, and spatial-temporal reference systems.

Our team focus on addressing critical challenges in Earth-Moon space exploration, Very Long Baseline Interferometry (VLBI), and spatial-temporal reference frameworks. We are committed to excellence in research, education, and technological innovation.

Through collaborative partnerships with international research institutions and industry leaders, we continue to contribute significantly to the advancement of surveying and mapping sciences.
